What is this & the whole workout

Why chords. Pop, film and cover arrangements are mostly chords, not running scales. The faster your hands recognise and grab a triad as one shape, the faster a lead sheet stops being note-by-note decoding. This workout builds that vocabulary the same way the scales do: a fixed plan you just run, a little more automatic each time.

The shape. A major triad is root + a note two steps up + another two steps up (1–3–5 of the scale). Right hand grabs it with fingers 1 3 5, left hand with 5 3 1. Same hand-shape in every key — only which keys are black changes.
