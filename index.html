Install on your iPad & add your own sheet music

Make it a home-screen app (so it opens full-screen and works without wifi):

  1. Open this page in Safari on the iPad.
  2. Tap the Share icon, then Add to Home Screen.
  3. Launch it from the new icon — it runs full-screen, and the lessons stay available offline once they've loaded once.

Add your own pieces / PDFs: drop the files into the practice-material folder and tell your teacher (the agent) the titles — they'll be added to the list above so they're a tap away at the piano.
